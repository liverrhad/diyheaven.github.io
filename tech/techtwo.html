Altoids Charger

Scroll to get building..

 

 

Never run out of Battery Again!

Materials:
- Altoids Tin
- Drill
- Car Phone Charger
- 9v Battery Clip
- 9v Battery
- Hot Glue gun

Step 1: Strip the Car Charger
 Remove the plastic housing for the circuitry of the car charger.

 Step 2: Drill A Hole
 Drill/cut a hole in the tin that is the size of the USB port and in the proper location.

 Step 3: Glue the Board
 Glue the circuit board into the tin. The port should fit snug into the hole you created.

 Step 4: Glue the Battery Clip
 Glue the battery clip to the car charger. The positive goes to the spring and the negative goes to the outer ring. Be sure that there is a solid connection between the components. After doing this, hook up the battery and it is ready to use.
