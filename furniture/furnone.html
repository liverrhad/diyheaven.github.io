White Wash Wood

Scroll to get building..

 

 

Helps add a Nice Touch to your Home!

Materials:
- White latex paint
- Water
- Rags
- Paint brush
- Sandpaper

Step 1: Clean the Furniture
 Remove all finishes that were on the wood via sandpaper. After thoroughly sanding, remove all dust that was left on the wood.

 Step 2: Mix the Whitewash
 The standard mixture for a whitewash is a 1:1 ratio between white latex paint and water.

 Step 5: Paint It and Wipe It
 Apply paint liberally and evenly over the entire area. After letting it dry for about 2-3 minutes, wipe it down with a dry rag. How much you wipe is up to you. Once you get the look you want, let the paint dry fully.

 Step 6: Apply a Finishing Coat
 Use a polyurethane clear coat to add a finished look to the piece while at the same time protecting the wood and the paint.
