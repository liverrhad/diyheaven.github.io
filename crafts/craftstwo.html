Lapel Pins

Scroll to get building..

 

 

Make Pins for every Outfit!

Materials:
- White Plastic Shrink Film
- Black Permanent Marker
- Fine grit Sandpaper
- Acrylic Craft Paint
- Bruch
- Water
- Pin Backs
- Hot Glue Gun

 Step 1: Lightly Sand Shrink Film
 Roughing up the surface will allow the paint to bond better. Be sure to wipe off any dust after sanding.

 Step 2: Trace and Paint
 Trace your desired design onto the plastic. Paint them with thinned out acrylic paint. Note: too much paint will cause unwanted clumping on the surface of the pin to be sure that the paint is thinned well.

 Step 3: Cut them out
 Cut out your designs. Cut as close as you can to the line so there is no white space on the outside.

 Step 4: Shrink them Pins!
 Place your shapes on a parchment lined cookie sheet and place in the oven for about five minutes at 250 degrees.

 Step 5: Add a Clear Gloss and the Pin Backs
 After the pins have cooled completely, add a clear coat of acrylic paint to the pins and glue the pin back onto the backs of the pins.
