Cups Out of Glass Bottles!

Materials:
- Glass bottles of your choosing
- Yarn
- Acetone
- Lighter
- Sandpaper

 Step 1: Create Ice Bath
 Fill your sink up with cold water and add several handfuls of ice.

 Step 2: Pour Acetone into a Dish
 Have just enough to soak the yarn and string.

 Step 3: Cut and Tie the Yarn
 Cut a piece of yarn long enough to be wrapped around the bottle 4 or 5 times. Wrap it around the bottle and tie the ends creating a sort of loop.

 Step 4: Soak the Yarn
 Soak the yarn in the acetone. Make sure it is completely soaked all the way through.

 Step 5: Put Yarn on Bottle and Light it on Fire
 Place the yarn on the bottle keeping the loop as tightly together as possible. Wipe off any acetone that got anywhere else on the bottle. Once it is lit, hold the bottle horizontally and rotate the bottle while it burns for about 30 seconds.

 Step 6: Submerge in Ice Bath
 Once the fame goes out, immediately submerge the bottle in the ice bath. The bottle should break into two pieces. If you donâ€™t get it on the first try continue to try until you get it. CAUTION: the edges of the glass will be very sharp.

 Step 7: Sand Edges
 Sand the edges of the glass until they are smooth. After getting rid of all of the glass dust, your glass is ready to use.
